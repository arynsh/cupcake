History of us

We are Daniel and Sharon! We live in the gorgeous city of Seattle with our three amazing kitties. We started baking cupcakes for our friends and family as a hobby but when they realized how amazing our cupcakes were, we became inundated with requests for custom cupcakes! This was when we realized that we could possibly make this hobby a full-time job! We're ecstatic that people love our cupcakes as much as we do!!
